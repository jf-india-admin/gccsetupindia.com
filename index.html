Why now?

Policy tightening around H‑1B visas makes it harder to bring talent to the U.S. Mid-size companies will increasingly build distributed teams and outsource work instead of relocating resources. Establishing a GCC in India gives you durable access to world‑class talent without immigration bottlenecks.

  • Resilience against visa and travel uncertainty
  • Timezone leverage for follow‑the‑sun execution
  • Lower cost base and faster hiring cycles

What is a GCC?

A Global Capability Center is your company‑owned, India‑based extension for engineering, product, data, finance, and operations—fully compliant and tightly integrated with your HQ.
